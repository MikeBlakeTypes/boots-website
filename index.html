ABOUT

Boots are Mike and Jason — two musicians who have spent almost three decades performing across Europe, from the most southerly point of the continent to the edge of the Arctic Circle, and just about everywhere in between.

From candlelit hotel bars to packed party floors to stadiums, the approach hasn't changed since 1997: real songs, played live, by a band that reads the room — and then turns it up.

Their versatility led to a seven-year residency on the Snowboard World Cup tour, performing wherever the competition travelled — from mountain summits and alpine nightclubs to ice caves, giant snowboard events and live international television.

Career highlights include performing during Salzburg's Winter Olympic bid, appearing before crowds of more than 50,000 at Nürnberg Stadium alongside Boss Hoss, and entertaining audiences numbering in the tens of thousands at Europe's Big Air events.
